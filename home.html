About Heartless

"Heartless" follows the story of a warrior battling not only powerful enemies but the darkness within himself. Inspired by the emotional turmoil of betrayal and resilience, this manga reveals the hidden struggles beneath a stoic exterior.

Meet the Creator

Created by Gandhi Muller, "Heartless" is a deeply personal work reflecting the battles we all fight inside. Combining stunning art and raw storytelling, this manga speaks to anyone who has faced heartbreak and chosen to rise again.
